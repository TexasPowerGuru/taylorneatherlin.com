About

Taylor Neatherlin grew up in Avoca, Texas — a town of 12 graduates in his senior class — where some of his earliest memories are opening gates on his father's oil leases, border collie in tow. That upbringing gave him something most accountants can't replicate: a firsthand understanding of the full petroleum and natural gas production cycle, from the wellhead to the balance sheet.

Taylor's upbringing and education has allowed him to understand the full spectrum of the petroleum and natural gas production cycle while representing the various and nuanced needs of each of his clients.

With over a decade of public accounting experience, Taylor has built long-term relationships with business owners, operators, and investors navigating complex tax situations — and the technical depth to handle whatever those situations require.
